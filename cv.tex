\section{Summary}
\small{As a developer I have a special interest in distributed systems and how software architectures and design choices can lead to boosts of performance or scalability issues. I enjoy exploring different paradigms and as such I'm quite fond of the actor model and the way it lets you reason about concurrency, as well as the functional paradigm due to its expressiveness and fundamental ideas that are also gaining traction in other languages. The main language I currently use to explore these concepts is Elixir.

Another topic that interests me is strong type systems. As expected from my formal methods background, I am concerned with safety and correctness issues and thus the ability to guarantee the absence of several classes of bugs at compile time really appeals me, hence my interest in languages like Haskell and Rust.}
