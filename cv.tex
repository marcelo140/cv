\section{Summary}
\small{As a developer I have a special interest in distributed systems and how our design choices can lead to scalable and maintainable systems. 

I enjoy exploring different paradigms and ideas. I'm fond of the actor model and the way it lets you reason about concurrency and failure. I'm also a fan of functional programming due to its expressiveness and other fundamental ideas that have been gaining traction by languages from other paradigms. The main language I currently use to explore these concepts is Elixir.

Another topic of interest for me is type theory. As expected from my formal methods background, I am concerned with safety and correctness issues and thus the ability to guarantee the absence of several classes of bugs at compile time is appealing to me, hence my interest in languages like Haskell and Rust.}
